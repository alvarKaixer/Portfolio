Philosophy
05

"When given an opportunity, deliver excellence and never quit. ― Robert Rodriguez"

I've been editing since I was around 10 years old, just messing around with random gameplay footage and uploading videos on YouTube. It was more of a hobby back then, just trying things out and having fun. During the pandemic, I came across AMVs (anime music videos), and that's when I really got into editing seriously. I started learning more about motion graphics, 3D animation, and eventually got comfortable with the software I still use today. After that, I began taking on commissions, from classmates at first, then eventually international clients. I kept doing it consistently over the years, improving with every project. Now, I'm at a point where I'm doing way more than I ever thought I could when I first started.
